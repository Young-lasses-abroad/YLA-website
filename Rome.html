In Rome you'll be spoilt with stunning architecture and art galleries, whilst not forgetting the amazing Italian food!

To narrow it down for you here's our lasses' Top 3...

#1: Trevi Fountain

Make a wish lass! The world-famous Trevi Fountain is an attraction not to be missed, tourists come from all over to toss in a coin and make a wish for good fortune.

#2: Visit the Vatican City

Did you know that the Vatican City is not actually in Rome!? Go say hey to the Pope and visit the stunning Sistine Chapel, all within the Vatican City which is it's own small country!

#3: The Coliseum

Live like the Romans for a day! The Coliseum is a world-famous public amphitheatre and one of Rome's greatest attractions to visit.

üöãSecondly, how do you get around to do all these amazing things!?

We know keeping yourself safe is a top priority when galavanting abroad but have no fear gals, there are plenty of options.

Here's our Top 3 recommended ways to get around Rome:

  1. Bicycle

    2. The cheapest and most effective to cut out traffic! Look like a local and keep yourself safe with this self-sufficient method of transport.

  2. Bus

    3. Rome's Public Transit Bus service runs 24/7 and covers the entire city, with multilingual drivers who can advise you on where to go.

  3. Metro

    4. Finally there is a very frequent and reliable subway service around Rome, also including a train service that takes you directly to Fiumicino airport.

Since you are on your hols don't worry about what areas to go to for the best and safest night spots, we've got you covered! Rome is known for a host of wines and cocktails to get your night started - did somebody say Prosecco!?

Here's our Top 3 areas to go for a boogie:

  1. Campo de Fiori

    2. A popular square in the centre of Rome surrounded by other tourists! It's a market by day and a bustling hub of bars and nightclubs by the evening!

  2. Piazza Navona

    3. Piazza Navona is the second central hotspot packed with nightclubs and close to local transport links, for dancing the night away!

  3. Monti

    4. Rome's up and coming 'Cool Quarter', loved by locals! You'll find a young crowd here, exploring cool bars and enjoying live music in the sun.
