In Santorini you'll be spoilt for beaches, great sightseeing and fabulous Greek cuisine!

To narrow it down for you here's our lasses' Top 3...

#1: Explore Akrotiri

Spend a day exploring the historic ruins of the city of Akrotiri.

#2: Visit Caldera Beach

Soak up the sun on Caldera Beach, famously known for it's small volcanic rock pebbles! But don't worry lass, you can walk barefoot.

#3: Santo Wines

Into your wine lass? Santo Wines is world-famous for it's selections of wines and snacks that you can sample with a great ocean-side view!
