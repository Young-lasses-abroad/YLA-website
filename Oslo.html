Oslo is one of Scandanavia's most expensive cities but also a gorgeous city that is situated along the Oslofjords.

To narrow it down for you here's our lasses' Top 3...

#1: Akershus Fortress

The Akershus Fortress is a medieval castle that was built for the royals in the 1290s. Since then, the fortress has been used as a prison and military base. The achitecture also provided inspiration for the Disney film Frozen which was also set in Norway.

#2: Opera House

Oslo Opera house showcases ballet and opera and is a must see when visiting Norway. The building itself also shows spectacular sites at the front of the fjords.

#3: Oslo Salt

The Oslo SALT is a hub for creative individuals to immerse themselves in art, music, food and architecture. Overlooking the Oslofjords are the prymaid wooden pannels and inside the building is a communal sauna area. To recieve the full experience of the sauna, those daring enough jump into the freezing cold fjord waters, climb back out, go back into the sauna and repeat.

    4. Like all Scandanavian countries, Norway has a superb cycling culture. But even better than cycling is the electric scooters which can be found all around the capital..
