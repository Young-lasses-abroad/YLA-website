About Us: We Are Young Lasses Abroad


We are a trio of girls who formed together to learn a new skill. Through the motto "coding is for girls too" we came together and created this website with the purpose to share our passion for traveling and through out own experience educate other female travellers out there on various aspects of travelling to cities in Europe.

You will find information on things to do, new foods to try and most improtantly how to stay safe whilst doing so, whether you're travelling solo or with a friend.

We hope you find the information useful and if you have a request of a city for us to cover next feel free to get in touch, we'd love to hear from you!
