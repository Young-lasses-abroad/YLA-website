Get to Manchester and feel the Northern soul of the UK with eclectic bars, restaurants, shopping and huge arts and cultural vibes!

To narrow it down for you here's our lasses' Top 3...

#1: Explore the Northern Quarter Makers' Market

Get your hands on some vintage and original clothing, artefacts and food in Manchester's eclectic Northern Quarter.

#2: Visit Didsbury

A short tram ride will take you to Didsbury, an up and coming area of South Manchester packed with lovely parks, shops and eateries.

#3: Visit HOME

We don't mean going home lass! If you're into your arts and theatre, HOME located in the city centre has a calendar of cool events 24/7, 365!

üöã Secondly, how do you get around to do all these amazing things!?

We know keeping yourself safe is a top priority when galavanting abroad but have no fear gals, there are plenty of options. We've collected the details about each particular city and what they have to offer!

Here's our Top 3 recommended ways to get around Manchester:

1. FREE Bus


Manchester has a very reliable FREE bus service around the city
from major train stations, just hop on and off as you need!

2. Tram


Tranport for Greater Manchester provides trams every 5-10 minutes both in
and around the city - just tap in with your card!

3. Train


There are four main train stations in Manchester city centre, so
hitching a train is fast and easy, including direct to Manchester Airport.
