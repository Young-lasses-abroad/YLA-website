üöã Secondly, how do you get around to do all these amazing things!?

We know keeping yourself safe is a top priority when galavanting abroad but have no fear gals, there are plenty of options. We've collected the details about each particular city and what they have to offer!

Here's our Top 3 recommended ways to get around Dublin:

1. Bicycle


The cheapest and most effective to cut out traffic!
Look like a local and keep yourself safe with this self-sufficient method of transport.

2. Luas Tram


Dublin's 24/7 tram network can get you in,
out and around the city. Including a direct line to Dublin Airport.

3. Leap Bus & Rail


Invest in a Leap card to take advantage of local buses, which can safely
get you to gorgeous rural areas further outside of the city.

Another famous square which stays alive straight into the night
