Paris, infamously called the city of love, or the city of lights. One of the most popular cities in the world brumming with an incredible art, fashion and food culture, there is something for everyone in Paris.

To narrow it down for you here's our lasses' Top 3...

#1: Eiffel Tower


Of course you can't visit Paris without seeing the norotrious Eiffel Tower. Although the
views from the top of the building are spectacular, the monument itself is a site to take in.

#2: Arc De Triomphe


The monumental statue honors French soldier who fought and died in the French
Revolutionary wars. Inscribed on the Arc are names of all French soldiers and generals.

#3: Cafes


Paris is full of quaint authentic cafes. Our favuorite by far however is Cravan, with it's
delicious food and pretty art decor.

We know keeping yourself safe is a top priority when galavanting abroad but have no fear gals, there are plenty of options. details about particular city and what they have to offer

Heres our top 3 recommended ways to get around

1. Bike

By far the cheapest way to get round Paris (other than by foot) is via the grey Velib bikes.
These are for rent and you are charged for use by the day.There are also multiple Velib
stations around the centre.


2. Metro

The Metro lines are the quickest way to get around Paris and runs efficiently on time,
however this is also the most crowded transporation method of choice.


3. Bus

The busses around Paris are well connected and they are a brilliant way to actually
see the city whilst getting around.
